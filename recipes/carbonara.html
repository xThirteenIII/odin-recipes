Ingredients

  • Pasta: 100g
  • Parmisan: 10g
  • Pecorino: 10g
  • Guanciale: 30g
  • Egg yolks: 2
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Meanwhile cook the Guanciale in a pan
  3. In a bowl, add egg yolks and mix them with a fork
  4. Add black pepper, parmisan and pecorino and mix
  5. Add some of the fat release from the Guanciale and mix
